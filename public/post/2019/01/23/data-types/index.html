2019/01/23

Vectors

Vectors are the basic building block of all data structures in R. All vectors have a length and type. There are no scalars in R only vectors of length 1. This lab will go through constructing them, subsetting them and then their various data types.

2                         #numeric vector of length 1
c(2,5,8)                  #numeric vector of length 3
1:5                       #numeric sequence of length 5
seq(from=1, to=9, by=2)   #numeric sequence by 2 
rep(2,5)                  #numeric repeated 
c('a'=1, 'b'=2, 'c'=3)    #named numeric vector
## [1] 2
## [1] 2 5 8
## [1] 1 2 3 4 5
## [1] 1 3 5 7 9
## [1] 2 2 2 2 2
## a b c 
## 1 2 3

Subsetting

You can select elements form a vector by position, value, or name.

x<- c('a'=3, 'b'=7, 'c'=13) 
x[2]
x[x==3]
x[x>5]
x['c']
## b 
## 7 
## a 
## 3 
##  b  c 
##  7 13 
##  c 
## 13
